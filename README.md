## About Me

I work at the intersection of **Vision-Language Models**, **multimodal retrieval**, and **data engineering**. My research focuses on negation understanding in CLIP-based systems — a fundamental gap in how VLMs handle language. I've published at **CVPR 2026** (GRAIL-V Workshop) and **ICPR 2026**, and I'm targeting WACV 2027 for my next submission.

Before grad school, I spent 4 years as a **data engineer and BI consultant** building production ETL pipelines and dashboards for Fortune 500 clients across retail and education verticals — work that grounds my research in real systems thinking.
